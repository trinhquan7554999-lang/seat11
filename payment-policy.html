Payment Policy

At Comfy Seat, we strive to provide secure, convenient payment options for our customers. This Payment Policy outlines the methods of payment we accept, our billing procedures, and how we protect your payment information.

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for all purchases made through our website:

Credit/Debit Cards

  • Visa
  • MasterCard
  • American Express
  • Discover

Other Payment Methods

  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Shop Pay
  • Bank Transfer (for orders over $1,000)

Payment Security

Your payment security is our priority. We implement several measures to ensure your payment information remains confidential and secure:

  • All payment transactions are encrypted using SSL technology.
  • We comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS).
  • We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Our payment processing systems are regularly audited for security compliance.

Billing Process

When you make a purchase on our website, the following billing process applies:

  1. You will see an itemized summary of your order before payment confirmation.
  2. After submitting your order, you will receive an order confirmation email with order details and payment information.
  3. Your payment will be processed immediately for credit/debit card and digital payment methods.
  4. For bank transfers, we will send you our banking details, and your order will be processed once payment is received.
  5. Once your payment is processed, we will send you an invoice by email.

Pricing and Currency

All prices displayed on our website are in US Dollars (USD) unless otherwise specified. Prices are inclusive of product costs but exclusive of taxes and shipping fees, which will be calculated and displayed during the checkout process.

We reserve the right to change prices at any time, but changes will not affect orders for which you have already received an order confirmation.

Taxes

Sales tax will be applied to orders based on the shipping destination's tax requirements. The applicable tax rate will be calculated and displayed during the checkout process before you complete your purchase.

For international orders, you may be subject to import duties and taxes when the product reaches your country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our product prices or shipping fees.

Invoices and Receipts

An electronic invoice will be sent to your email address after your order has been processed. You can also access your order history, including invoices, by logging into your account on our website.

If you require a specific format for your invoice or have other special billing requirements, please contact our customer service team before placing your order.

Payment Issues

If you encounter any issues with payment processing, such as declined transactions or double charges, please contact our customer service team immediately at +15754893196‬ or via email at payments@comfyseat.com. We will work with you to resolve any payment issues as quickly as possible.

Policy Updates

We may update this Payment Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices, payment options, or regulatory requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.
