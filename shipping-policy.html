Shipping Policy

At Comfy Seat, we're committed to delivering your premium stool safely and promptly. This Shipping Policy provides information about our shipping methods, delivery timeframes, and related procedures.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays) after payment confirmation. You will receive an order confirmation email with your order number once your order has been placed, and a shipping confirmation email with tracking information when your order ships.

Please note that during peak seasons (such as major holidays) or promotional periods, order processing may take up to 3 business days.

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states, Canada, and select international destinations. If you have questions about shipping to your location, please contact us before placing your order.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team.

Please allow up to 24 hours after receiving your shipping confirmation for tracking information to become active in the carrier's system.

International Shipping

For international orders, please be aware of the following:

  • Import Duties and Taxes: International orders may be subject to import duties and taxes levied by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping fees or product prices.
  • Customs Clearance: International shipments may experience delays due to customs processing. We have no control over this process or any associated delays.
  • Address Accuracy: Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate, including any postal codes, to avoid delivery issues.

Shipping Delays and Issues

While we strive to ensure timely delivery, certain factors beyond our control may cause delays:

  • Severe weather conditions
  • Natural disasters
  • Carrier service disruptions
  • Customs delays for international shipments
  • Incorrect or incomplete shipping information

If you haven't received your order within the estimated timeframe, please check the tracking information. If there appears to be an unusual delay or problem, please contact us, and we'll help resolve the issue.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

We take great care in packaging your stool to ensure it arrives in perfect condition. However, if your package arrives damaged:

  1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and/or product.
  2. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  3. Provide your order number and photos of the damage.

For lost shipments, please contact us if the tracking shows delivery but you haven't received your package, or if there has been no update to the tracking information for an unusual period.
