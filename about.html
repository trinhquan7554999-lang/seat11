About Comfy Seat

Our Story

About -- Comfy Seat was born in the heart of 2010 by a few passionate furniture craftsmen who had one simple goal in mind: Create the absolute most comfortable seating solutions without sacrificing an inch on quality or style. From humble beginnings as a small workshop in Furnituretopia, a nationwide chain of department stores specializing in home furnishings, to now one of the most recognised brands in the furniture industry, we take pride in the fact that our signature International Concepts 1S-818 18-Inch Scooped Seat Stool is now available across 50 states in the USA! We have grown but never compromised on our promise of quality craftsmanship. Each stool is still hand-tested when they leave our workshop meaning that when you buy a Comfy Seat product, you know your furniture has been made to last.

Our Values

Sustainability

We are also committed to sustainable practices, sourcing our wood from responsible suppliers and minimizing waste in our production. We want to make beautiful furniture that does not compromise on the future of our planet.

Quality

Everything we do is rooted in quality. At 361Studios, we believe that furniture should be timeless, sculptural, and well crafted, which is why we do not sacrifice materiality and technique when creating each piece.

Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We believe in every product we sell and provide outstanding customer service to make sure you are 100% satisfied with your Comfy Seat purchase.
